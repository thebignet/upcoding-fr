My goal is to determine the best solution available in response to digital needs. Agile and software crafting methodologies allow me to build software from the ground up.

Heavy upfront design can be cumbersome, difficult to change and ultimately ending up far from user's needs. Agile methodologies favoring communication and rapid feedback are key to building software in a lean fashion.

As a software developer, I collect business needs in order to design a digital solution from it. I am able to model the business' domain using best practices from Domain Driven Design.
